MALIBU

SLEEPHAWK

My debut single as Sleephawk - exploring the interweaving of technology and romance with the title Malibu referring to malibu beach - a metaphor for an ideal instagram post location.

Each piece in this series was created using my unique collage method and style, aiming at creating surreal fragmented spaces that reflect the overlaying of feelings and images that we are bombarded with by technology.
