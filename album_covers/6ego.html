EWBTDOY 2020

SLEEPHAWK

My first album Ego Will Be the Death of You 2020 written in a time of mental health struggle, but also creative growth, with this being the year I learned how to design with Blender 3D. The whole album was finished during 2020 Covid as the strange side-effect of the time was having a lot of time to be creative, which led me to finish off all the songs that peppered the previous year.

This design is actually taken from a ureleased music video, which matched the Disconnected aesthetic but extended them into a realm of video game horror characters and symbols. I decided it was too ambitious as a full video, but the scenes made it into the Spotify canvases for each song, giving an extra dimension to the tone and world of the album.
