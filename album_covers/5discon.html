DISCONNECTED

SLEEPHAWK

My first single, Disconnected, as sole producer on Sleephawk, written in a time of mental health struggle, but also creative growth, with this being the year I learned how to design with Blender 3D.

This shot is taken from the music video, which explores a world in which people are rebuilt from a state of physical fragmentation. The video took about a year to learn and create, which was an incredible learning experience, whilst also a stark light on how much work being a musician/ songwriter / producer / designer / animator is.
