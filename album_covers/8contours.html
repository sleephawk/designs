CONTOURS

KING COMPASS

For the EP Contours by King Compass - my six piece dream-folk band. The project was short-lived and well loved, with unique shows in just-as-unique venues, such as a sold-out EP launch in basement space designed as a cave, a stage at Sonisphere festival and a Sofar Sounds set overlooking London.

The concept for the EP was around self-discovery and reflecting over others, with that being represented with a map on a face. The piece is made simply with fineline ink.
