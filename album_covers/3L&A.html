LOVE & ANXIETY

SLEEPHAWK

This EP was written around the complexity of dating with anxiety or an anxious disposition, exploring self-sabotage, dating apps and overthinking. The 4 track EP's cover was made at the same time as Glass, creating two figures with complex, unreadable identities represented by fragments in place of faces. The song Tabby, (All My Friends Were Right) marked Sleephawk's first radio play on BBC Radio 1 Intro and BBC Radio 6, featuring long time collaborator Aymee Weir.

Each piece in this series was created using my unique collage method and style, aiming at creating surreal fragemented spaces that reflect the overlaying of feelings and images that we are bombared with by technology.
